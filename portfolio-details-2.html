Welcome to the Esp 8266 NodeMCU projects page

1)SMART ATTENDANCE SYSTEM

Smart Attendance System is an automated projects system that records the attendance of employees or students getting out and in an istitution, This can be applied to large organisations and companies which employ staff in big numbers or to learning institutions such as universities which has many attendance in a day. These project needs,Esp 8266 NodeMCU,RFID Reader,XAMPP Database Software.

2)HOME AUTOMATION SYSTEM

This project Uses Arduino Mega, Sound Sensor,DHT11 Temperature Sensor,DC Motor,LED bulb systems in the house, by lighting when the temperature around the house goes above a preset temperature,for example when the temperature rise 30 degrees Celcius, a fan turns on, cools the room till it falls to or below the preset temperature. In the lighting system, the switching on and off of the bulbs around the huse can be controlled by certain gestures, such as clapping of hands.

3) SMART SECURITY SYSTEM

Smart Security System focuses on the security in and around the house, and could be used for effective detection of trespassers. A sensor is used in the detection of movement, and relays the message to a phone that alerts the owner that a motion has been detected around the house.The project requres ,Esp 8266 NodeMCU,PIR Sensor and A telegram bot that relays the telegram message to your phone
